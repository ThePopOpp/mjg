You may opt in at:

[SMS Opt-In](https://my.michaeljgauthier.com/sms/opt-in)

You may opt out at:

[SMS Opt-Out](https://my.michaeljgauthier.com/sms/opt-out)

### Mobile Information and Messaging Consent

**No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Text-message originator opt-in data and consent will not be sold, rented, traded, or otherwise shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes.**

We may disclose mobile information and messaging-consent records only to vendors, telecommunications carriers, messaging aggregators, platform providers, and other service providers that need the information to deliver, route, secure, support, or document MJG communications. These providers are authorized to use the information only to perform services for MJG, comply with law, prevent fraud or abuse, or maintain messaging operations—not for their own independent marketing.

After an SMS opt-out request, we may send one final confirmation message. We will not send additional SMS marketing or program messages unless you later provide new consent, including by using the SMS Opt-In page.
