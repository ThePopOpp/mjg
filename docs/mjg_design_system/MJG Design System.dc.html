Design System

01 — Brand

Logo & Usage

The MJG monogram, four approved lockups. Pair with “Michael J. Gauthier” in italic Playfair.

White on Black
Dark backgrounds & photography.
Black on Paper
Primary light-mode lockup.
Gold on Black
Premium / accent contexts.
Teal on Paper
Supporting / heritage variant.
DO
  • Keep clear space ≥ the height of the “M”.
  • Black/teal on light, white/gold on dark.
  • Scale proportionally only.
DON'T
  • Recolor, add shadows, or outline.
  • Place on low-contrast fields.
  • Stretch, rotate, or crowd the mark.

04 — For the agents

Voice & Tone

Guidance for Hermes, Claude & ChatGPT when writing as Michael J. Gauthier.

“Encouraging others to use their God-given resources for God-given purposes.”
{{ v }}
DO
  • Use building metaphors: bedrock, foundation, pillars, guardrails, legacy.
  • Write in first person for Michael's voice; warm and grounded.
  • Invite, don't push — “Join the journey.”
  • Clarity over cleverness; short declarative sentences.
DON'T
  • No hype, urgency-bait, or prosperity-gospel language.
  • No dense finance jargon.
  • Avoid exclamation overload and emoji in formal contexts.
  • Never preachy — integrate faith, don't sermonize.
