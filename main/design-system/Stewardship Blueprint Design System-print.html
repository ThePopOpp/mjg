Section 01 · Foundations

Brand foundations

Three things make a Stewardship Blueprint surface feel like a Stewardship Blueprint surface: the MJG monogram, the disciplined type rhythm, and a single ✦ used as the brand's heartbeat. Everything else is restraint.

Tone
Reflective, plain-spoken, steady.
Faith-based but never faith-exclusive.
Hype, urgency, motivational shouting.
Cadence
Short sentences. Plenty of white space.
One idea per paragraph. One question at a time.
Stacked claims, paragraph blocks, jargon.
Posture
Invitation. "You are welcome here."
Honest, but never accusatory.
Pass/fail framing, guilt, fear-of-missing-out.
The Mark

The ✦ system

The four-pointed star is the brand's quiet signature. It marks moments of pause, reflection prompts, eyebrow labels, and section transitions. It's a directional star — pointing readers back toward intention.

Use it sparingly: one per section, one per card, one before a label. Never more.

Section 02 · Color

A palette built on warm restraint
