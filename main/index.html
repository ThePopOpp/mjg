The Blueprint

What Is the Stewardship Blueprint?

Most people build their lives by accident — chasing urgency, accumulating success, and one day asking why it doesn't feel like enough. The Stewardship Blueprint is a guide to doing the opposite.

Structured like a building, the Blueprint starts with bedrock — a foundation of faith and identity — and works outward through the pillars of a well-designed life: family, fitness, fun, and finances. Each layer is reinforced by guardrails, habits, and energy, culminating in a legacy worth leaving.

I'm writing it chapter by chapter, and you're invited to join me from the very beginning.

Author · Financial Planner · Life Designer

In 2013, I stepped away from a secure career on Wall Street to pursue something more aligned with my values. That leap taught me that success without purpose is hollow — and that a meaningful life doesn't happen by accident.

I'm now writing The Stewardship Blueprint to share what I've learned about designing a life that integrates faith, family, finances, and legacy into something whole.

The Wake Up Call

Where We Begin: Parts I & II

Before you can build anything meaningful, you need to wake up to why it matters. These first two chapters are that wake-up call.

01

Created for More

Life is often lived by default, not design. Most of us race through our days without pausing to ask whether the life we're building is the one we actually want. Chapter 1 begins with an honest look at how easily we drift.

  • Success does not equal purpose
  • Living by default vs. living by design

02

Why Most People Build the Wrong Life

Even when we're paying attention, we often start with the wrong blueprint. Chapter 2 explores the trap of chasing urgent over important — and how misaligned priorities quietly shape a life we didn't intend to build.
